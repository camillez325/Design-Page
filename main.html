Sign In and Start Listening

You can sign in or sign up by navigating the starting page. Email verification is required for new customers. For the main searching functionality, just search for either song names or artist names like your everyday player app!

Build Playlist with Tags

You can build their on the fly playlist by adding hashtags to songs. Then you can search for certain songs by entering hashtags on the search bar. To enter multiple hashtags, just enter '#' one after another!

Share Playlists with Friends

What's more?! You can create your own music community by following your friends and letting your friends follow you on Tagify. You can also select your following list to include your friends' hashtags in your own playlist by navigating to the 'Tags' section on the bottom. That way, your friends playlist will also be part of your searching results!
